AtomSiteSusceptibility¶

atom_site_susceptibility_moment was added according to suggestion of Henrik Thoma <h.thoma@fz-juelich.de>:

Since a large number of orientations and magnetic fields measured for one compound in the weak ferromagnetic state, it is introduced an additional weak ferromagnetic moment tensor to refine data.

atom_moment_i = (moment_ij + abs(field) * chi_ij) * field_j
